About Us

Wisdom Academy was established in 2015 with a mission to provide quality education and inspire a culture of learning. By 2017, the school had grown into a strong community dedicated to both teaching and student development. Over the years, Wisdom Academy has become a trophy-winning institution, proudly ranked 1st out of 84 schools in Bole Sub-City. In 2025, the Academy expanded with a new campus for Grades 1–6, offering modern classrooms and a nurturing environment designed to help young learners thrive. Today, Wisdom Academy continues to shape bright futures through excellence, innovation, and commitment to every student’s success.

Our Mission

To be recognized as best school in Ethiopia based on the curriculum and police of the land , provide adequate knowledge ,skill, information, talent , attitude, vision and values for those student enrolled all grade and level of education in Wisdom Academy.

Our Vision

Enabling the rising generation, by way of quality education, so as to become product, society competent, problem solvers, developing will rounded personality with human characters and values to be able to face the challenges of the 21st century in education.
