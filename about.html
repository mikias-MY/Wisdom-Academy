About Wisdom Academy

Founded in 2015, Wisdom Academy has a clear mission: to provide high-quality education while fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. From the beginning, the school has focused on holistic student development, ensuring growth academically, socially, and emotionally.

By 2017, Wisdom Academy had grown into a vibrant learning community where teachers and students collaborated to achieve excellence. Its commitment to quality education earned recognition and set the stage for future achievements.

Over the years, Wisdom Academy has become a trophy-winning institution, proudly ranked 1st out of 84 schools in Bole Sub-City. In 2025, a new campus for Grades 1–6 was opened, featuring modern classrooms, safe learning spaces, and an environment designed to help young learners thrive.

Today, Wisdom Academy continues to shape bright futures through innovative teaching, strong values, and personalized attention. Every day, the school inspires minds, builds character, and nurtures the leaders of tomorrow.
